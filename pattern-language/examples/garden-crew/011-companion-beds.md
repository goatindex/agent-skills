# 11. Companion Beds

…within the ground laid by the
[Shared Plot Charter (1)](001-shared-plot-charter.md), new members need a way
into the crew's knowledge, not just into its soil…

## Problem

**Newcomers learn fastest beside an experienced grower, but pairing people
formally as "mentor and novice" embarrasses both and rarely survives a
month.**

A hypothesis from watching one garden: the pairs that lasted were never
announced — they emerged from adjacent beds whose stewards could not help
talking. The failed formal buddy scheme at the same garden lasted three
weeks. Untested beyond that single observation, hence no asterisk.

## Therefore

**Assign a newcomer's bed physically adjacent to an experienced steward's
bed, and say nothing else. Let the pairing be a fact of geography, not a
role.**
