# First season

Purpose: stand up a new garden crew from nothing, in the order that keeps the
agreements ahead of the disputes.

Entry conditions: you have land, at least four committed people, and one
growing season ahead. If you do not yet have four people, recruit first —
this sequence assumes a crew, not a founder.

1. [Shared Plot Charter](../001-shared-plot-charter.md) — write it at the
   first working bee, before any soil is turned; the charter is easy while
   nothing is yet at stake.
2. [Bed Stewards](../010-bed-stewards.md) — assign stewards the same day the
   beds are marked out, so no bed is ever unanswered-for.
3. [Companion Beds](../011-companion-beds.md) — place any newcomer's bed
   beside an experienced steward's when allocating.
4. [Weekly Water Roster](../020-weekly-water-roster.md) — the roster goes up
   the day the first seed goes in, not the first hot week.

Cautions: do not skip step 1 because the founding group feels like friends —
the charter exists for the members you have not met yet. And do not run this
sequence mid-dispute; settle the dispute first, then charter what you learned.
